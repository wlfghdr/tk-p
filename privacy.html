Privacy Policy

Timekiller fully respects your privacy.

No Data Collection

This app collects NO personal data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any information about you.

Local Storage

All your settings and highscores are stored exclusively locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the app

Stored Information (local only)

  • Player name (optional, entered by you)
  • Game settings (language, theme, grid size, etc.)
  • Highscores (Top 10 game scores)

No Tracking

We use NO:

  • Tracking technologies
  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party SDKs
  • Cookies or similar technologies

No Network Connections

The app works completely offline and makes NO connections to external servers.

Children's Protection

Since we collect no data, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children.
